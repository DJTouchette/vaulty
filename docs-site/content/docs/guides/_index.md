# Guides

Practical walkthroughs for the things you'll actually want to do with Vaulty. No fluff, just the good stuff (okay, maybe a little fluff).

- **[MCP Setup]({{< relref "/docs/guides/mcp-setup" >}})** — Connect Vaulty to Claude Code, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible agent.
- **[Policy Templates]({{< relref "/docs/guides/templates" >}})** — Pre-built configs for Stripe, OpenAI, AWS, and more.
- **[Team Sharing]({{< relref "/docs/guides/team-sharing" >}})** — Share a vault with your team without sharing your passphrase.
- **[OS Keychain]({{< relref "/docs/guides/keychain" >}})** — Save your passphrase so you stop typing it.
- **[Framework Integrations]({{< relref "/docs/guides/frameworks" >}})** — Import from .env files, Rails credentials, Docker Compose, and Kubernetes.
- **[Cloud Backends]({{< relref "/docs/guides/backends" >}})** — Pull secrets from AWS, GCP, HashiCorp Vault, or 1Password.
